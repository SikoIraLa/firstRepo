Welcome to our Studio.



From user research and wireframes to final design, our design experts employ industry best practices to achieve high user engagement and build user friendly website designs, mobile and desktop applications. Design Thinking has never been this transformational.






Zerminate Inc. is a creative design studio which crafts beautiful and intuitive user interactions leading to higher customer engagement. Our major focus is prototyping and making your products market ready.







Design Systems and Branding



UI/UX Design



We prototype and build products in a human-first way. The interfaces are designed with a balanced focus to both Aesthetics and Functionality.



Design Systems



Our Design Systems are preferred over any traditional brand guidelines because our assests tell stories that sell. Identifying USP for client's products and capitalizing them has never been easier.





Component Based Designs


With products being component oriented these days, Zerminate Inc. also makes design component based. This will bring uniformity over branding and marketing of different products.
